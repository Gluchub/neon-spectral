About について

The Concept

Neon Spectral is where the supernatural meets cyberpunk. This immersive experience blends the ethereal aesthetics of Ghostwire: Tokyo with the neon brutality of Cyberpunk 2077.

Navigate through a spiritual plane overlapping with a futuristic Tokyo, where digital yokai and corporate spirits haunt the electrified landscape.

The Technology

Built with cutting-edge WebGL technology, Neon Spectral pushes the boundaries of browser-based experiences.

Featuring GPU-accelerated particle systems, procedural animations, and interactive audio design that responds to your every movement.

Experience 体験

Spirit Vision

Activate your spectral sight to reveal hidden yokai entities lurking in the digital shadows. Interact with spirits to unlock hidden messages and pathways.

Neon Seals

Draw mystical kanji seals to bind and exorcise cyber-ghosts. Each seal creates unique particle effects and sound responses.

Phantom Gates

Navigate through traditional torii gates that serve as gateways between the physical and digital realms, each revealing a new layer of the cyber-phantom world.
