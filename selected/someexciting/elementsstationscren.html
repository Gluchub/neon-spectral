Neon Spectral

A Cyber-Phantom Interactive Experience where the digital and spiritual realms collide. Navigate through a world of neon-lit torii gates and spectral entities in the heart of a cyberpunk Tokyo.

The Ethereal Merge

In the year 2099, the boundary between the digital and spiritual worlds has dissolved. What remains is a city where neon lights illuminate ancient shrines, and digital ghosts haunt the networks that power society.

Visitors describe feeling a strange presence - digital yokai that manifest as glitches in reality, visible only to those who've been touched by the network's spectral energy.

The Neon Spectral project documents these phenomena through an interactive journey into the heart of this cyber-spiritual convergence.

Phantom Experience

Move through layers of reality as you explore the Shibuya Crossing transformed by the spectral convergence. Each layer reveals new entities and mysteries to unlock.

The digital yokai respond to your movements, either fleeing from your presence or drawing closer to investigate this visitor from another plane of existence.

Digital Exorcism

Spirit Vision

Activate enhanced perception to see beyond the veil of reality. The deeper you go, the more the spirits will reveal their true nature and intentions.

Shrine Gates

Pass through digital torii gates to transition between layers of the spectral realm. Each gate serves as a portal to areas with different spiritual densities.

Phantom Vanish

Master the art of navigating the boundary between worlds, allowing you to phase in and out of spiritual detection.
