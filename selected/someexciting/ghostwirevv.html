The Vision

Neon Spectral merges the mystical Shinto-inspired world of Ghostwire: Tokyo with the raw tech dystopia of Cyberpunk 2077.

In this realm, ancient spirits wander through neon-drenched streets, where technology and the supernatural collide in a haunting dance of light and shadow.

Explore a world where digital ghosts haunt the network and paranormal forces manipulate the very fabric of reality.

Key Features

Spectral Encounters

Interact with digital yokai that react to your presence, revealing hidden messages and paths.

Neural Interface

Navigate through a 3D representation of Tokyo's haunted cyberspaces using intuitive gestures.

Kuji-kiri Magic

Master ancient finger weaving spells to unlock digital gates and banish hostile entities.

Immersive World

Our WebGL-rendered environment creates a haunting fusion of physical and digital realms, where neon signs flicker with supernatural energy and spirits manifest through glitching interfaces.
